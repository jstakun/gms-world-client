New in version 0.4:

  • Deals Calendar menu item - see what deals are valid today or until any other day. Go to Menu -> More -> Deals calendar,
  • Deal of the day - receive deals recommendations from us. You'll need to use Deals Anywhere for some time before you'll receive any deals recommendations,
  • Deal of the day menu item - go to Menu -> More -> Deal of the day and see deal recommended for you,
  • Recently opened deals menu item - go to Menu -> More -> Recently opened,
  • My locations menu item - go to Menu -> More -> My locations and see your locations history,
  • Rate us menu item - rate our app at Google Play. Go to Menu -> More -> Rate Us,
  • Recently opened deals highlighted on the map view,
  • Wide Android versions support from v1.5 to v4.0,
  • Quick deals search,
  • Deals sorting by rating,
  • Google Analytics integration.

New in version 0.3:

  •  Share deal button - If you want to share selected deal details via Facebook, Twitter, email, SMS or any other application click on share button and select application from the popup list,
  • Built-in deal preferences analytics.

New in version 0.2:

  • Deal Categories preferences - go to Menu -> Settings and enable/disable deals categories,
  • Hot Deals button - click on Hot Deals button and see Deals of the day.

Auto Check-In let you automatically check-in at your favourite places.
  • How it works? - In order to be checked-in automatically you have to add place to Auto Check-In list and meet following conditions:
    • Your current distance from the place is less than 0,2 kilometer (~0,12 mile),
    • Last check-in at the place has occured more than 8 hours ago,
    • Since last check-in you have been in distance of at least 1 kilometer (0,6 mile) from the place.
  • Adding place to Auto Check-In list - Select landmark in map view and click check-in button. Landmark will be added automatically to Auto Check-In list.
  • Removing place from Auto Check-In list - Go to Menu -> More -> Check-in -> Auto Check-In list and long press on the place. Select delete from the context menu.
  • Disabling/Enabling Auto Check-In - Auto Check-In is enabled by default. In order to disable/enable Auto Check-In go to Menu -> More -> Settings and enable/disable Auto Check-In checkbox.
